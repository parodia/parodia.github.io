Fatih(parodia\parody\illusion); He was born in May 1989. In 2016, he received his "Master's Degree" in Painting from the Social Sciences Institute. He is still waiting to be appointed to the relevant departments of universities.(x)

Although he had a great interest in Digital Media for many years, he tried to establish a conceptual integrity between "Canvas Media", which can be felt as traditional, and "Digital Media" as part of the modern one. He still continues to work on randomness and "Generative Art", so to speak.
